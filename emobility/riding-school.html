Our Training Program Structure

Beginner to Test-Ready Path:

  • Weeks 1-2: Basic controls, balance, safety equipment checks, and slow-speed maneuvers.
  • Weeks 3-4: Understanding traffic rules, road awareness, and practical riding in controlled environments.
  • Weeks 5-6: Advanced maneuvers, hazard perception, and initial mock tests.
  • Final Week: Intensive DVLA test preparation and final mock exams.
