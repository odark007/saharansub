What is Launchpad Lab?

It's not just about ideas — it's about rolling up your sleeves, testing, failing, iterating, and finally building something real or for the fun of it.

Launchpad Lab is the "Y Combinator before Startup School" — a hands-on training where kids and teens learn to think, build, and pitch like innovators. Whether you want to bootstrap an idea from scratch or practice how to talk to venture capitalists, Launchpad Lab gives you the playground and guidance to explore.

Our Philosophy

Learning happens through doing. We don't just teach entrepreneurship — we let kids experience it.
